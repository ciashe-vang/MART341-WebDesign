Sweetroll

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup caster sugar
  • 1 cup maple syrup or honey
  • 3 eggs (beaten)
  • 1 1/2 cup butter (melted)
  • 2 vanilla pod (scraped)
  • 3 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1 cup icing sugar

Directions

  1. Cream the butter and sugar together and beat until fluffyoF.
  2. Slowly add in each chicken's egg and beat well. Add the milk or cream.
  3. Add the flour, salt and vanilla extract and stir gently until mixed.
  4. Pour into greased swell roll pans and bake until golden, about 20 minutes. Allow to cool.
